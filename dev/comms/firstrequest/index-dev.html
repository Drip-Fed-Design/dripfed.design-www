Your first request

While we're just finishing setting things up, we thought we'd share some details with you on how you can make your first design request!

As we mentioned in your welcome email, we use Wrike for all tasks and projects. Wrike provides us with an all-in-one platform for managing projects, organising tasks, enhancing collaboration, and accelerating execution.

To make submitting design requests as easy and flexible as possible, we've provided two ways for you to achieve this.

Two ways to submit a request

Create using an online form

Option 1 - This will be the most flexible and easiest way to submit a new request for most people.

Click the link to your form, which includes predefined questions, and simply fill in the gaps then click submit.

Once we've received your request, we'll add it to the Request Table and start working on it. Give it a try!

Create a card directly

Option 2 - This option offers a more hands-on approach to creating new requests.

Within your Wrike Request Table, hover the cursor over the column to which you'd like to add a new request and click the '+ Item' button.

We also have a handy template that you can duplicate or use as a reference.

Have a question or want to discuss something outside of a request, or just fancy a chat?

No problem. We actually have a handful of different ways you can get in touch.

You can reach out to us directly through Wrike. Alternatively, you can contact us via WhatsApp and email.
